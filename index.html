Roguelike Dungeon Crawler using Rot.js

Read more about Rot.js

The villagers have been complaining of a terrible stench coming from the cave.

Find the source of this smell and get rid of it!

Controls:

[,] to pick up items
[d] to drop items
[e] to eat items
[w] to wield items
[W] to wield items
[x] to examine items
[;] to look around you
[?] to show this info in-game

Characters:

  • @ - you
  • F - fungus
  • B - bat
  • : - newt
  • k - kobold
  • Z - giant zombie(boss)
  • s - slime
  • % - food
  • * - corpse
  • ) - weapons
  • [ - armor
